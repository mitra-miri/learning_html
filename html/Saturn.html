More info

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun. It is famous for its beautiful rings. The rings are made of ice and rock. Saturn is a gas giant. It is the second largest planet. A day on Saturn is 10.7 hours long. One year on Saturn equals 29 Earth years. Saturn has over 140 moons. Titan is its largest moon. Saturn is the least dense planet. It would float in water.
